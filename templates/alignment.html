Alignment

Important: You should update values shown on this page to fit your organisation.

Key priorities made by your organisation is outlined on this page. This must be done to ensure alignment between your organisation and your risk management efforts. The prioities will affect how all your risks are prioritised, so chose them carefully. Impact priority is the organisations priorities of different risk types. Changing the priority will change how threats are scored and prioritised. Higher priority means higher weight in the risk score.

Impact levels are used when assessing threats. Impact levels describes the differences between low, medium, high and critical levels.

NOTE: You change the impact priorities and levels by manually editing the impact_description section of the assessments/data.json file.
